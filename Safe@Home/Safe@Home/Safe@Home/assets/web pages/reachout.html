Athlone House of Strength Athlone House Of Strength is a shelter for abused women and their children. The shelter offers services that include counselling as well as school services for women residing at the shelter in addition to school services to the broader community. Rosary, Charleston Hill, Paarl, 7646 021 862 9983
Carehaven Centre Carehaven Centre is an emergency intake shelter for abused women and their children that provides therapeutic support services. Salvation Army Carehaven, Gatesville, Cape Town, 7764 021 638 5511
Philisa Abafazi Bethu Philisa Abafazi Bethu is a 24-hour emergency shelter service for women and children who are victims of sexual- and domestic violence. The shelter supports and educates women and children about their rights. In addition the shelter empowers residents with entrepreneurial skills through income generating activities. 91 Grindal Avenue Lavender Hill, Cape town,7945, South Africa 081 746 9889 (Office hours) or 084 521 2897 (after hours)
Creative Effective Families Creative Effective Families is a shelter for abused women and their children. The services offered by the shelter includes: counselling service , accommodation and basic needs , child therapy , assists victims with protection orders , trauma debriefing , counselling for substance abuse , life skills training D'almeida, Alhof Dr, Fairview, Mossel Bay, 6506 044 693 1092 (24 Hour Line)
Sisters Incorporated Sisters Incorporated was established in 1959 as a home for pregnant, single women and teenagers considering adoption, as well as a safe place for teenagers with a detention order. In the late 1980s Sisters Incorporated opened its doors to victims of domestic violence and survivors of rape. While the shelter continues to provide housing for pregnant, single women considering adoption it does not accommodate teenage girls who are not accompanied by their mothers. 32 Ascot Rd, Kenilworth, Cape Town, 7708 021 797 4190
